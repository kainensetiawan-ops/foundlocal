# FoundLocal — Platform Strategy (verified 27 Jun 2026)

## The core insight: INBOUND vs OUTBOUND
- **INBOUND ("gigs")** = you list a service, clients browse and BUY directly. No chasing, no per-bid cost. → This IS "clients come to me, AI does the work."
- **OUTBOUND ("bidding")** = you chase job posts, spend ammo (bids/Connects). Limited, slower.
- **Doctrine: lead with INBOUND everywhere; use bidding only surgically.**

Lucas does: clicks submit/publish, uploads, ID/KYC, replies to messages I draft.

## 🥇 FIVERR — best fit, our #1 horse (already set up)
- Free, NO pay-to-apply, NO approval gate. Pure inbound gigs.
- **7 gigs** allowed as New Seller (L1=10, L2=20, Top Rated=30). Make 7 narrow ones.
- **New-gig boost**: ~30-day visibility window (strongest first 24-48h). Sprint for 3-5 orders + reviews before it decays.
- Ranking = relevance (same 2-4 keywords across 80-char title [keyword FIRST], 5 tags, 1,200-char desc) × performance (reply <1h, deliver early, reviews).
- Fee: **20% flat.** Earnings clear 14 days after completion (7 if Top Rated). Withdraw: PayPal (~$1 min), Payoneer (~$20 min).
- Price Basic low to break cold-start. Add a gig video where possible.
- AI: NOT required to disclose AI use (only hard bans: deepfakes, imposters, reselling identical bulk AI output). Add genuine human work per order.
- Briefs (free) exist but favor Success Score 7+ → lean on gig SEO + boost early, not Briefs.

## 🥈 UPWORK — use Project Catalog, skip the bidding
- The wall = **Connects** (pay-to-bid): only 10 free/month, ~2-6 per proposal. Don't grind it.
- **Project Catalog** = free inbound gigs (like Fiverr), no Connects, no badge — once approved. Up to 20 projects, 3 tiers each. ← our Upwork play.
- **Approval gate**: Upwork rejects generic profiles. Apply with a SHARP niche (AI automation / web dev / video editing), 100% profile, 3+ portfolio pieces. Avoid "general freelancer/VA."
- Fee: variable **0-15%** per contract (usually ~10-13%, shown before you accept). Withdraw: Direct-to-Local-Bank MYR ~$0.99.
- ⚠️ **Circumvention = instant permanent ban**: never take a client/contact off-platform for 24 months (the real rule — NOT a "30% penalty"). 13.5% conversion fee if you ever legitimately move one off.
- One account, real legal name. ID verification (MyKad/passport) can gate proposals + withdrawals.
- Specialized Profiles being retired after ~28 May 2026 — don't build them.

## Honest expectations
- New + zero reviews = slow first order (days to ~4-8 weeks). Budget TIME, not money.
- Paid promotion (Fiverr Promoted, Upwork Boost) is locked for new accounts → first orders rely on FREE levers only (gig SEO, fast replies, Project Catalog).
- Real take-rate on small orders is higher than headline (Fiverr ~24-35% all-in; Upwork ~12-15%+ with FX). Price the NET, not gross.
