About

A designer who codes, and a developer with an eye for type.

For nearly a decade I've helped startups and studios turn fuzzy ideas into products that feel inevitable. I sit comfortably between design and engineering — which means fewer handoffs, fewer compromises, and a final result that's true to the vision.

My approach is quiet and deliberate: understand the problem, strip away the noise, and obsess over the small things most people only feel. The best work, I think, is the kind you never notice working.
