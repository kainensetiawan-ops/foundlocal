What we do

A complete studio, end to end.

From the first conversation to the final pixel, we operate as one focused team — no handoffs, no noise.

01 Brand Identity & Strategy Positioning, naming, visual systems and the story that holds it all together.
02 Digital & Web Design Editorial websites and products designed and shipped with engineering rigour.
03 Art Direction & Editorial Photography, type and layout for print and screen, with a point of view.
04 Motion & Interaction Considered movement that brings brands to life without the gimmicks.
The Studio

We are a team of twelve designers, writers and engineers who believe the best work comes from fewer, deeper partnerships — built on trust, craft and an obsessive eye for the detail nobody else notices.

Founded in 2014, FOUNDLOCAL works with a handful of clients each year — from emerging founders to established cultural institutions. We don't chase scale. We chase the kind of work that still feels right a decade later.
