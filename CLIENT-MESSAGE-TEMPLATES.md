# FoundLocal — Client Message Templates

Human, warm, no AI-slop. Swap [brackets] each time. For real inquiries, JARVIS writes a bespoke reply — these are the instant fallback.
RULE: keep everything ON the platform (Fiverr/Freelancer/Contra). Never share email/WhatsApp before a contract.

---

## 1. New inquiry (someone messages before ordering)
> Hey [name], thanks for reaching out! 👋
> Happy to help with [their thing]. Quick few questions so I get it right:
> 1. What's your business / what does it do?
> 2. Do you have a site already, or starting fresh?
> 3. Any sites or styles you like the look of?
>
> Tell me a bit and I'll point you to the right package — no pressure either way.

## 2. "Which package should I get?" 
> Good question! Based on what you've told me, [Basic/Standard/Premium] is the best fit — it covers [the 2-3 things they need]. If you want [extra thing], the [next tier] adds that.
> Want me to send you a custom offer with exactly what you need? That way you only pay for what fits.

## 3. The free-preview close (for someone on the fence) ⭐
> Tell you what — I know it's hard to trust a new seller. So here's what I'll do: send me your business name + what you do, and I'll mock up a quick preview of your homepage **for free**. If you love it, we make it official. If not, no hard feelings and you owe nothing.
> Fair? 🙂

## 4. Order kickoff (right after they order)
> Awesome, thanks for the order [name]! 🙌 Excited to build this for you.
> To get started, can you send me:
> - [content/text for the pages]
> - [logo + brand colors if any]
> - [photos, or tell me if you want stock]
> - A site or two whose style you like
>
> The more you send, the closer my first draft lands. I'll have something for you by [date]. 🚀

## 5. Delivery
> Here's your [website/video] — really happy with how it came out! 🎉
> [what's included / how to use it]
> Take a look and let me know if you'd like any tweaks — your revisions are included, so don't be shy. Want it perfect for you.

## 6. Review request (after they're happy)
> So glad you love it! 🙏 If you've got 20 seconds, a quick review would mean a lot — I'm new here and building up, so it genuinely helps. Either way, it was a pleasure working with you. Reach out anytime you need more!

## 7. Scam / spam deflect (the natalyjack-type messages)
> Thanks, but I keep all work and payment on Fiverr for both our safety — happy to help if you place an order through the platform. 👍
(Then: Report → Block. Never click their links.)

---

## Tone rules
- Warm, confident, plain. No "Dear Sir/Madam", no "I am passionate about leveraging…".
- One idea per message. Short. Emojis sparingly (1-2).
- Always end with a clear next step or a light question.
- For a REAL inquiry, JARVIS reads it and writes a tailored reply — paste-ready.
