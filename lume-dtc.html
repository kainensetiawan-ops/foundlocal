Three steps

From box to calm in
under a minute.

Fill & blend

Pour in water to the line, add 6–8 drops of your Lume botanical blend, and twist the ceramic lid back on.

Set the mood

Tap once for mist, hold for the glow. Pick a 2, 4, or 8-hour timer — or let adaptive mode decide.

Let it breathe

Lume reads the room and keeps it gently scented. It shuts off on its own when the water runs low.

Adaptive mode

It learns your room, then gets out of the way.

No app required. The sensor ring reads humidity every few seconds and adjusts output — full when you walk in, soft while you sleep.
